Our Mission

Food waste is a serious issue, and is only getting worse as time goes on. A third of all food produced, equivalent to 2.5 billion tonnes, is wasted every single year.

In consumer homes, overbuying, poor planning, confusion over labels and safety contribute to food waste. With well-informed choices, we can change this!

Recipeasy aims to help fix this. Find step by step recipes that use a combination of ingredients already in your fridge!

Eat a unique and delicious meal everytime, and find creative recipes that help you reduce your personal food waste and save you some money.

What is Recipeasy?

Simply press the "try it out" button to make some delicious food with what you already have! Our website will match the ingredients you type into your search bar to an a recipe that's a perfect fit for your fridge.

Get started now to create the most Recipeasy meal!
